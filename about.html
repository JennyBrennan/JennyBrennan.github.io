About Jenny

I'm a designer/developer living in London.

I started a website design business when I was 14 after teaching myself to code aged 11.

I've spent a lot of time helping others learn to code, through workshops, online and in schools.

I've also worked on marketing and communications, from startups to the NHS and internationally.

My academic backround is in Politics, Philosophy and Economics, and I have a particular interest in the effects of unconscious bias in hiring processes.

When I'm not doing those things, I'm usually found advocating for effective altruism (can I recommend this book?) or behind a drum kit.
